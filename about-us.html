Our Restaurant

Bringing the flavors of India to Australia

Welcome to Biryani House - where the tantalizing aroma of spices and the rich flavors of Indian cuisine come together to offer you an unforgettable dining experience.

Our restaurant is a family-owned business that has been serving authentic Indian food for over a decade. Our signature dish, the biryani, is made with fragrant basmati rice, a blend of aromatic spices, and your choice of succulent meat or fresh vegetables.

We take pride in our use of fresh and locally sourced ingredients, which are carefully selected by our skilled chefs to create a perfect balance of flavors.

Memorable Dining

Warm and inviting restaurant that is perfect for a casual meal with friends and family, or for hosting a special event.

Quality and Service

Whether you choose to dine-in or order take-out, our team is committed to providing you with the highest level of service.
